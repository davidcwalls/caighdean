In the case of the prepositions *de*, *do*, *faoi*, *i* and *รณ*, the preposition merges with the article in the singular as shown below:

| de + an   | &#x21E8; | den     |
| do + an   | &#x21E8; | don     |
| faoi + an | &#x21E8; | faoin   |
| i + an    | &#x21E8; | sa, san |
| รณ + an    | &#x21E8; | รณn      |

</table>
